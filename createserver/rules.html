SERVER RULES

  • No Hacking/Unfair Advantages
    This includes things such as resourcepacks, mods, etc.
  • Keep griefing and stealing minimal
    The server may be mostly chill, but stay respectful and nice.[1]
  • Voicechat groups must be kept open
    When creating a group in Simple Voice Chat, make sure the group type is set to "Open" so that other people can still hear you from outside of the group.
  • Keep destructive PvP away from everyone else
    Have a designated spot to fight, or make sure the way you're fighting doesn't affect anyone outside of the problem. Tanks, powerful bombs, missiles, etc. are not allowed anywhere near 'civilians.'
  • Weapons not usually allowed have exceptions
    You can use these weapons for non-harmful purposes such as mining, farming, or anything that doesn't affect people in a negative way.[2]

    • CLAUSES

  • [1] Scamming is a mode of stealing.
  • [2] Highly powerful weapons, like nukes, must be tested 1,000s of blocks away from everyone, noting their destructivity and stability, to make sure you don't underestimate how much damage it will deal.
