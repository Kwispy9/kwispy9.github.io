SERVER RULES (reading warning)

  • No Hacking/Unfair Advantages
    This includes things such as resourcepacks, mods, etc.
  • Keep griefing and stealing minimal
    The server may be mostly chill, but stay respectful and nice. (Exceptions can be made in war)
  • War is allowed
    You can declare war with anyone, and any amount of people. War can be declared through as little as a piece of paper to as much as dealing repairable damage to the other party's property. (irrepairable damage includes, but is not limited to: killing pets, farm anaimals, or slaves, destroying built structures to the point of unrecognizability, etc.) repairable damage, for example (and preferably), would be creeper-sized craters or similar. If you cause too much damage, accidentally or not, the declaration is automatically denied and you will be required to compensate (ex. repair with your own funds).
  • Keep the Geneva Convention in mind
    No nukes, overly destructive items, or war crimes of any sort. If you want to keep the server alive and active, the obvious way to do that is to not destroy.
  • More about war
    If someone declares war on you, and you don't want to fight, you can't just deny them. They may give you options out of the war, and you must choose one. If they don't give you any options, then that's too bad.
    Wars cannot happen in public areas; they have to have a designated area for them to happen, away from everyone else in case of cross-fire, and to prevent ugly terrain. During war, powerful weapons are allowed, such as tanks and bombs, but not nukes and things listed in the Geneva Convention rule.

    WAR CRIMES

    The following is a list of war crimes that are not allowed on the server

  • Nuclear weapons
  • Attacking/killing an unarmed player (on purpose)
  • Attacking a hospital
  • Using a hospital as a shield
  • Treating war prisoners badly; like they're not humans
    • This list, and the rules above, are not set-in-stone and can be edited/added to at any time (you will be notified when that happens)
