4D-SMITH [04/30/2025]

At the start of the dream, that I can remember, I was playing some weird mix of 4D-Miner and Planetsmith. It had the multiplayer of 4D-Miner, but the worlds of Planetsmith, and it also had voicechat. And then, somehow, my oldest sister joined in with me, but my desk became a mess all of the sudden, so I got up to clean it. When I came back, I saw my brother at my second-oldest brother's desk with a switch, playing what looked like the game I was playing.
But, when I asked him, in an interrogative tone, what he was doing, he said he was "playing a level," but I still somehow thought he was on my computer, messing with my things on the game, so I got mad and told him to get off. He, of course, got confused and mad, and I apologized, explaining the misunderstanding, but when I did, I said I was dreaming? (in the dream, I wasn't talking about the actual dream, but I guess I felt like I was dreaming inside the dream) It was weird.

After that, my second-oldest sister commented on how destroyed my headphones were, and I told her they were the same as the last time she saw them. My oldest sister was also now on my computer, but I didn't mind that for some reason.

Then my niece came over, and that's about where the dream ends.

DREAM LOG #2 [05/02/2026]

The dream started off with being at some sort of store, or place where we werepicking up packages. It was me, my oldest brother, his girlfriend, and my second-oldest brother. We were in a parking lot as we were packing things into the car; the things were for ym niece and my mom. For some reason, the way I was packing it was dumping everything into one big box, even though I was thinking that we wouldn't know whose stuff is whose, but I carried on anyways.
When it was done, we debated how it should be put in the back of the car, sicne there was also other things in there as well.

Then we were suddenly in my oldest brother's apartment, no transition as far as I can remember. My niece was there, too, and then we all started playing Minecraft. However, it was like I was in the game itself, but I knew I was just playing it. As we were playing, it seemed like my niece was getting some sort of specioal treatment, like she was part of something. As it would turn out, it was for a special ritual of sorts, but I'm not sure what the ritual was for.
It quickly became a serious thing, where the "demon" of the ritual required "another child," which we knew was my niece, buit we refused. This would be after my niece said she was getting sores all over her body and stuff. Then, somehow, I got separated from everyone, and my niece was gone. ike really gone. I struggled with mobs in the world, like ghosts and other things that were born from the ritual. Once I finally managed to get back to the waypoint I set earlier for our base, I wasn't sure if I put the waypoint some way off from the base, and didn't remember which way to go from there, or if the base just completely disappeared. But when I got to it, there was this beautiful woman there who told me I won't be able to get my niece back. I didn't believe her, and I tried making a jump across a big drop, but I missed and died of fall damage. When I respawned, I'd have had enough, so I just teleported back to the waypoint. When I teleported, I collected my stuff instantly, but I was falling, so I aimed for some water and landed in it.
As I was making my way out of the water, I saw something, I don't remember what, that got me madder than I already was, and said to the woman something along the lines of, "I hate you! All you witches are bad!" To which the woman replied, "I am a good witch. You need to see something, look," and then I was woken up by a loud noise that just turned out to be my brother getting down from his bed, but I was thinking, as a joke, maybe the "good witch" woke me up, and that's what she wanted me to see.
